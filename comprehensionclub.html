ABOUT COMPREHENSIONCLUB

Established in 2017, COMPREHENSIONCLUB has trained over a 1000 students, shaping their careers and building success stories. In Noida, COMPREHENSIONCLUB has been training students for national and international tests such as the CAT, GMAT, GRE, CET, SNAP and NMAT for well over a decade.

With an exceptional faculty team, COMPREHENSIONCLUB Noida Mentors have more than 10 years of training experience between them, and have themselves taken the CAT, GMAT and the GRE. The faculty, backed by the most comprehensive study material, training programs and centres across the city, make IMS Pune the first choice for aspirants in the city pursuing programs from elite institutions in India and abroad.
